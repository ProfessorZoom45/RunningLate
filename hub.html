Game of the Week

Pick'em Rewards

Members vote on the weekly showcase game in Discord, then correct picks become end-of-year awareness rewards for true freshmen.

Display The Matchup

!gotw posts the active Game of the Week, deadline, vote totals, and pick options pulled from the league spreadsheet.

Cast A Pick

!gotw v home or !gotw v away logs each member's winner guess, replacing their previous pick for that same GOTW.

Check Results

!gotw results shows the winning side and correct-pick results once the weekly showcase game is final.

Freshman Reward

Correct picks earn awareness points. Only true freshmen can receive the reward, with a max of +10 awareness to one true freshman.

Spreadsheet Source

GOTW data is designed to pull from the Game of the Week, GOTW Votes, and GOTW Rewards spreadsheet tabs.
